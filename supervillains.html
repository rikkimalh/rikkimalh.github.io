Mustard Man		 Wields condiments as weapons Many condiment based puns. Bad ones
Kite Man Chuck Brown He flies with a giant kite on his back. Most of his apprehensions are the result of him crashing into a tree.
Star Conquerer Starro the Conquerer Finally an odd villain with ACTUAL POWERS! Taking on the appearance of a star fish and then passing his powers on to other star fish who would then go on to absorb the power of atomic bombs and scientists brains.
Codpiece Unknown Has a giant transforming weapon attached to his groin. Transformations include a cannon,missiles, sonic attack, boxing gloves and tools such as drills and scissors. You can't makethis stuff up. Or can you? He adapted the name codpiece when he was made fun of for his height. Interpreting that tomean the size of his manhood, Codpiece was born.
Crazy Quilt Paul Dekker Has a helmet that hypnotizes his victims. He also has a fascination for quilts. His appearance in the Lego Batman movie!
The Eraser Lenny Fiasco Special helmet eraser, able to run out evidence. Covering the tracks of other crimes, and for a 20% cut he will erase the evidence of another crime.
Onomatopoeia Unknown A serial killer who imitates every noise around him, from gunshots to dripping faucets. He once shot Deadshot in the head from point blank range, but didn't kill or harm him. Nor did he realize he wasn't dead as Onomatopoeia overlooked Deadshot's ability to play dead.
King of Cats Karl Kyle He dresses up as a cat... Copying his sister, Selina Kyle, Karl decided to become a less attractice cat villain.
Penny Plunderer Joe Coyne None, he just likes stealing pennies. If you've ever watched any Batman cartoon or movie, remember that giant penny that is in the Batcave? Batman got it from the Penny Plunderer as a souvenir.
Ten-Eyed Man Philip Reardon Can see out of his finger-tips (get it? ten eyes!) After being caught by Batman and escaping multiple times, he is finall yput in jail and his hands are locked in a special box to keep him blind all day and night.

Honorable mentions

  • Tweedledee and Tweedledum, Alice in Wonderland based characters who bounce around. They don't do much else.
  • Blue Snowman, who is not actually a snowman but rather a woman posing as a man posing as a blue snowman.
  • Rainbow Raider, who is colorblind and emits rainbow beams to ride on and attack his foes. Yes, you read that right.
  • Egg Fu, a giant living egg with a really nice mustache.
  • The Prankster, who (you guessed it) pulls pranks.
  • Polka-Dot Man, who committed bizzare crimes revolving around polka dots.
