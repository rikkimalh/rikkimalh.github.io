Danny The Street None, he is an actual street A living sentient piece of urban geography who can magially and seamlessly place himself in any urban landscape. His name is a pun on female impersonator Danny La Rue ('La Rue' meaning the street in French.)
Mogo None, it's(?) a planet. No, really He has willpower. A member of the Green Lantern Corp, Mogo is responsible for bringing green lantern rings to their new host after the prior host dies.
Red Bee Richard Raleigh, Jenna Raleigh Richard did not have any superpowers, but carried a 'stinger gun'. Jenna posessed a human/insect biology which granted her enhanced srength and the ability to mark people for later tracking. The character only became popular when re appearing in the DC Comic 'All-star Squadron, where Richard was killed by Baron Blizkreig.
Vext Patron Deity of Mishap and Misfortune A god who causes misfortune and bad luck to others but has no control over this ability. Being banashed to Earth after him and other gods were phased out. He ended up renting a room in the same building as The Heckler.
